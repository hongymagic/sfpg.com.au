Sales Property Management Advisory: Purchases

For many of our vendors, selling their home or investment is one of the most financially significant times of their life. So when you sell with SFPG, we treat the sale of your home with professionalism and service tailored to you and your property.

When selling your property with SFPG, the main objectives to be achieved are:

  • Providing you with an accurate assessment in the sale price of your property
  • Selling your property within the specified timeframe requested by you
  • Maximising the sale return of your property in current market conditions
  • Honest and continuous feedback and updates during the sales process
  • Making the sales of your house seamless and stress free
