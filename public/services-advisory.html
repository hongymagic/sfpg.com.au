Sales Property Management Advisory: Purchases

As a first time buyer or investor, you may not know the property values or property types in an area. As a sophisticated investor, you may not have the time to do the appropriate due diligence.

SFPG can recommend researched areas or properties that lie within your budget, financial position and real estate objectives.

When you are looking to purchase a property, SFPG can perform the customised research and due diligence regarding pricing, rental yields or market conditions of the area.

So when you purchase a property, SFPG research aims to provide you:

  • Customised research for your potential property
  • Market condition reports for your desired area
  • Accurate and reliable advice to ensure a successful real estate decision
  • Independent and analytical advice for your potential property
  • Comprehensive strategies to ensure a stress free purchase
  • Personalised approach to purchasing at the right price
