About The Future of Real Estate Staff Profile

SFPG embraces technology and recognise its importance as a strategic marketing tool and knowledge bank.

The power of the internet has reshaped the real estate industry. As the World Wide Web, brings people and real estate closer together, the definition of local agents have changed.

Knowledge of an area that was previously known only to the local real estate agent is now easily accessible due to technology. Properties are now advertised to a global audience. The large majority of potential purchasers and potential tenants are found via the internet. At SFPG, we never focus on a particular area but we focus solely on our clients. We provide the platform for our clients to make informed real estate decisions.
