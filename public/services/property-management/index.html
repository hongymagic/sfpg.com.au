Sales Property Management Advisory: Purchases

The choice of an agent to manage your property is extremely important. A bad agent can adversely affect your return for investment, while a good agent will only increase your return and the value of your property.

When you invest into SFPG to become your managing agent, we will add value to your property by:

  • Minimising the vacancy period
  • Maximising the rental return on your property in the current market
  • Finding the most suitable and responsible tenant for your property
  • Ensure your property is well maintained and looked after by the tenant
  • Ensure that rent is collected on time and not in arrears
  • Ensure that the rend is reviewed at the end of each lease term

Whether you are a first time investor, sophisticated investor or international investor, SFPG will provide a comprehensive strategy suited to all your needs.
